Getting Started

  • Call to set up a tour of our facility: Contact
  • Sign a Release of Information Form so we can obtain records from your doctor
  • We will review your records and confirm your eligibility for enrollment
  • Our Social Worker will visit your home and explain more about our program
  • You will be scheduled for two free visit days to fully determine if our program is right for you
  • After your visit days, we will schedule assessment days and complete the enrollment process
